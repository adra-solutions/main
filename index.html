Purpose & scope

Canadian Tax Estimator is a personal tax-planning tool designed to help you estimate Canadian federal and provincial/territorial income taxes. You can select a tax year and province/territory, enter your income and deductions, and view estimated total tax, after-tax income, and average/marginal tax rates.

The goal is to help with planning and “what-if” scenarios (for example, seeing how an additional RRSP contribution could reduce your estimated taxes and change your estimated tax return/refund).

Important notice This application provides tax estimates only and is not a substitute for professional tax software or advice. Do not use these estimates for filing an actual tax return.

Privacy & data collection

The app is intended to be helpful without requiring you to create an account. Depending on your platform and configuration, the app may use third-party services (for example, advertising) that can collect limited device or usage information.

What data may be involved

  • Advertising: if ads are enabled, ad providers may collect identifiers or usage data required to serve ads.
  • Diagnostics: the operating system or app platform may collect crash logs/diagnostics depending on device settings.

The exact data collected (if any) depends on the version of the app and your device settings. Refer to the platform privacy prompts and the app’s store listing for the most current disclosures.

Limitations

  • Estimates can differ from official CRA calculations and professional tax software.
  • Tax rules, credits, and rates can change; results may vary by year and jurisdiction.
  • The app may not account for every personal tax situation (for example, specialized credits, business income, or complex tax scenarios).
  • Always verify results using official CRA resources or a qualified tax professional.
